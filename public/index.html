Welcome to Shelby Protocol

This app turns your daily discipline into a game. You start at Rank 15 and work your way up to Rank 1 — Thomas Shelby — by completing daily tasks consistently.

The more you show up, the higher you climb. Miss days and your rank drops. There are no shortcuts.

How to earn XP

  • +1 XP — Complete a task
  • +5 XP — Complete ALL tasks in a day
  • +2 XP — Submit a daily reflection
  • +1 XP — Open the app daily

Reach Thomas Shelby in 30 days

Complete all tasks, reflect daily and check in every day — you will reach Rank 1 in exactly 30 days. Miss days and it takes longer.

Daily Non-Negotiables

These are your daily tasks — things you MUST do every day. No excuses. No skipping.

Tap a task to mark it complete. Tap again to unmark it.

Adding Tasks

Tap + Add Non-Negotiable to create your own custom tasks. Each task gives +1 XP when completed.

Daily Reset

Tasks reset every day at midnight. If you don't complete all tasks your streak resets to 0 and you lose XP. Stay consistent.

All Tasks Bonus

Complete ALL tasks in one day and earn a +5 XP bonus. This is how the best climb fast.

The Rank System

There are 15 ranks. You start at the bottom — Street Rat — and earn your way to the top — Thomas Shelby.

Ranks are based on your total XP. Earn enough XP and you automatically rank up.

Rank Drops

Miss tasks and lose XP. Lose enough XP and your rank drops. This is accountability — the Shelby way.

The Goal

Reach 360 XP to become Thomas Shelby. A perfect 30 days gets you there.

Earning Badges

Badges are awarded for milestones. Once earned they are yours forever — even if your rank drops.

Badge Types

  • 📅 Check In — Open the app for the first time
  • ⚡ First Blood — Complete your first task
  • 🔒 Full Lockdown — Complete ALL tasks in one day
  • 🔥 Streak Badges — 3, 7, 14, 30 day streaks
  • 💪 Task Badges — Complete 10, 50, 100 total tasks
  • 🏆 Rank Badges — Reach each new rank
  • 👑 Thomas Shelby — Reach the top rank

Tommy's Words

Every day Tommy Shelby gives you a personalised message based on your rank, XP and progress. Powered by AI and speaks directly to you.

Tap Get Today's Orders to receive your daily message.

Daily Reflection

Answer one reflection question per day. Be honest. Each reflection earns +2 XP.

After 7 reflections you earn the Deep Thinker badge.
